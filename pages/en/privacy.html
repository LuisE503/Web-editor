Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Important: CodeCraft Studio is a fully client-side application. All your projects and data are stored locally in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data or projects to our servers.

1. General Information

CodeCraft Studio ("we", "our" or "the application") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our web application.

2. Information We DON'T Collect

CodeCraft Studio DOES NOT collect, store, process, or transmit:

  • Personal information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Your source code or projects
  • Usage or behavioral data
  • Location information
  • Tracking cookies
  • Unique user identifiers

3. Local Storage

The application uses your browser's local storage (localStorage) to save:

  • Your projects: The HTML, CSS and JavaScript code you write
  • Preferences: Theme (dark/light) and language preferences
  • Auto-save: Automatic backups of your current work

All this data remains solely on your device. It is never sent to any server. You have full control over this data and can delete it at any time by clearing your browser data.

4. Third-Party Libraries

CodeCraft Studio uses the following third-party libraries:

  • CodeMirror: Code editor (loaded from CDN)
  • Font Awesome: Icons (loaded from CDN)

These libraries are loaded from public content delivery networks (CDN). Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

5. Analytics and Cookies

CodeCraft Studio DOES NOT use:

  • Google Analytics or other analytics tools
  • Tracking cookies
  • Tracking pixels
  • Third-party scripts for advertising or tracking

6. Security

Since CodeCraft Studio is a client-side application that does not transmit data, the security of your projects depends on:

  • Your web browser's security
  • Your device's security
  • Your own security practices

We recommend keeping your browser up to date and using appropriate security software on your device.

7. Code Execution

The code you write is executed in an isolated iframe (sandbox) within your browser. This code is never sent to any server. Code execution is entirely local and under your control.

8. External Links

Our application may contain links to external websites (e.g., documentation, social media). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. We recommend reading the privacy policies of any website you visit.

9. Minors

CodeCraft Studio is not specifically directed at children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not intentionally collect information from minors.

10. Changes to Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.

11. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data that we can provide, modify, or delete. You have full control over all locally stored data in your browser.

To delete all locally stored data:

  • Chrome: Settings ‚Üí Privacy and security ‚Üí Clear browsing data
  • Firefox: Options ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Clear data
  • Safari: Preferences ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings ‚Üí Privacy, search, and services ‚Üí Clear browsing data

12. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us through:

  • Email: privacy@codecraft.studio
  • GitHub: github.com/codecraft-studio
Summary: CodeCraft Studio fully respects your privacy by not collecting any data. Everything you create stays on your device under your complete control.
